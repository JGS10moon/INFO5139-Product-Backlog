INFO5139-Product-Backlog

GitHub Repository:

User stories are tracked using GitHub Issues and a GitHub Project board.

Product Backlog:

Academic Planning & Scheduling

Academic Scheduling & Planning
US-001: As a student, I would like to create a weekly schedule to effectively organize my class times, assignment due dates and study time.
US-002: As a student, I would like to view all my courses and deadlines in one dashboard to easily see and prioritize my academic workload.

US-003: As a student, I would like to be able to add and edit assignments in my schedule to ensure my academic plan remains up to date throughout the course.

Time Management & Workload Control
US-004: As a student, I would like to receive updates and reminders for upcoming deadlines to avoid forgetting to complete assignments or make time to study for exams.
US-005: As a student, I would like to have an estimated time needed to complete assignments and study times to plan assignment completion and study times realistically.

US-006: As a student, I would like to break large assignments down into smaller mini assignments to manage my workload.

Task Breakdown & Flexibility
US-007: As a student, I would like to see my workload for the week to avoid overloading myself.
US-008: As a student, I would like guidance on building a study routine to improve my time management skills.

Progress & Routine Building
US-009: As a student, I would like to adjust my weekly schedule so that I can adapt to changes academically and personally.
US-010: As a student, I would like to see visual indicators when completing assignments to know what I have completed and track my progress through the course.

Wellness, Stress & Self-Care:
Stress check-ins
US-11: As a student, I want to explore tools to manage stress levels to balance my mental health on the right path.
US-12: As a student, I want to know places or support options for extra help and consolation to my schedule.

Wellness tracking 
US-13: As a student, I want a way to track my schedule to find the perfect balance between life and school.
US-14: As a student, I want a system that keeps track of my breaks, mental health consulting and building confidence when completing certain notions.

Self-care reminders
US-15: As a student, I want to be able to set reminders for important events in relation to mental health and scheduled tasks or counseling.
US-16: As a student, I want to be reminded of schedule bookings and tasks giving the option to select if completed or not.

Stress vs workload connection
US-17: As a student, I want to build Connection between work, life and school to manage all at once with a mixture of schedules, mapping & tracking, in an easy format.
US-18: As a student, I want simplistic styles that won’t bring me more stress to manage on top of what I already have.

Exam-period support
US-19: As a student, I want support when it comes to studying in exam weeks and quizzes to help me understand what I should improve on.
US-20: As a student, I want to learn tasks to help manage my grades in a proper manner.

Smart Features, Goals & Business Value:

Smart Routines & Automation
- US-021: As a student, I want smart routine suggestions based on my class schedule and habits so that my weekly plan fits my real life.
- US-022: As a student, I want my routine suggestions to automatically adjust when my schedule changes so that I don’t have to rebuild my plan every week.

Goal Setting & Motivation
- US-023: As a student, I want to set daily, weekly, and monthly goals so that progress feels manageable and achievable.
- US-024: As a student, I want to see visual indicators such as streaks or momentum for my goals so that I stay motivated by consistency rather than perfection.

Intelligent Task Prioritization
- US-025: As a student, I want the app to automatically prioritize my assignments based on deadlines and workload importance so that I focus on the most critical tasks first.
- US-026: As a student, I want the app to automatically enable a focus mode during scheduled study times so that I can reduce distractions and stay productive.

Collaboration & Communication
- US-027: As a student enrolled in a group project, I want an automatically created group chat with shared announcements so that all group members receive important updates in one place.
- US-028: As a student working in a group, I want to see a shared progress bar based on defined progress points so that everyone stays aligned and accountable.

Premium Learning & Focus Support
- US-029: As a premium user, I want short, optional micro-learning check-ins based on upcoming lecture topics so that I can quickly assess my understanding before tests and exams.
- US-030: As a premium user, I want the app to forecast upcoming workload importance and reorder my tasks so that I always work on the most impactful items.
